## Haiku Series 2 “4 Seasons”
**By Kris Yotam**
**11/30/2024**

### Springs Awakening
It begins again
This Hope Renews, Grows, and Transforms
Giving us longer days

### Summers Vibrant Hues
With Joy it appears
Its glory shines we behold
Nourishing our skin

### Winter's Silent Peace
Bringer of joy
Its far to much fun
The snowflakes glisten
